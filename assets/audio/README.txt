This folder is empty on purpose — no copyrighted music is bundled.
(There is no background music player on this site — just a few short sound effects.)

Add your own short mp3 files here and the site will pick them up automatically:

  blow-candles.mp3          -> plays when the interactive cake candles are blown out
  happy-birthday-tune.mp3   -> plays when all candles are lit
  soft-chime.mp3            -> plays when the gift box is opened

If a file is missing, the site fails silently (no console errors, no broken UI) —
it simply won't play a sound until you add one.

To use different filenames, edit the src="..." attributes on the <audio> tags
near the top of index.html.
